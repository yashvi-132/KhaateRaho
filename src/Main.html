Cooking Tips and Tricks

1.] Crack your eggs on a flat surface to avoid getting bits of the shell in your food.

2.] Season every step of the cooking process to prevent over-salting at the end...

3.] But if you do add too much salt, throw a quartered potato in to tone down the flavor and absorb some of the excess.

4.] Use pasta water to create a pasta sauce.
If you thought your pasta tasted good before, wait until you try this trick. Before dumping used pasta water, add a cup of it to your sauce pan. Then, add the pasta directly into the skillet. The salty water will add more flavor to your dish. Your taste buds will appreciate it—and the texture will be spot on.
